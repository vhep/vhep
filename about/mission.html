Goal, Vision and Mission

Goal

To add more value to healthcare systems from which all stakeholders can benefit.

Vision

To improve effectiveness and efficiency across multiple stakeholder levels by providing evidence-based and patient-focused research and education to students,  researchers, health providers, practitioners and policy makers.

Mission

To draw and extend upon the existing research and policies of value based healthcare by fostering a collaborative, multi-disciplinary, local-global environment which encourages novel strategies to address real world health problems by maximising the skills, expertise and resources of the university and its affiliates, and creates lasting positive impact.
